Akan Names

The Akan people of Ghana frequently name their children after the day of the week they were
born and the order in which they were born which have a meaning according to the character.

Day of the week Female Male
Sunday Akosua Kwasi
Monday Adwoa Kwadwo
Tuesday Abenaa Kwabena
Wednesday Akua Kwaku
Thursday Yaa Yaw
Friday Afua Kofi
Saturday Ama Kwame
